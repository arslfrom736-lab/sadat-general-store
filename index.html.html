About Us

Learn about our journey and commitment to serving the G-12 community

A Legacy of Trust & Quality

Sadat General Store has been a cornerstone of the Aslam Market community in G-12, Islamabad for over a decade. Founded by Asghar Shah, our store began with a simple mission: to provide residents with access to high-quality daily essentials at fair prices.

What started as a small shop has grown into a trusted neighborhood destination, thanks to our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and authentic products. We understand the importance of reliable service in daily life, which is why we carefully curate our inventory to meet the diverse needs of our community.

From premium Basmati rice and pure cooking oils to baby care essentials and household necessities, we ensure every product meets our strict quality standards. Our customers aren't just transactions—they are our neighbors, friends, and extended family.

Asghar Shah - Owner "Our success is built on trust. When you shop at Sadat General Store, you're not just buying products—you're investing in quality that your family deserves."
